Chemenigma

Explore the science of chemistry through a scientist's eyes - where there is no precise problem, no clear experiment and only vague theory. Take your own decisions and conduct experiments until you find your answer. After qualifying for the experimental stage, participants will be presented with problems directly impacting the daily lives of people and will have to find innovative solutions to them. They will then explain to scientists the strengths and compromises of their approach. This event is a true representative of the challenges a scientist today faces. Come see for yourself!
